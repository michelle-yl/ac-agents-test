# Serial Dilution in a 96-Well Plate

1. Prepare diluent in a reservoir.
2. Add 180 µL diluent to wells B1–H1 (row-wise serial dilution).
3. Add 200 µL sample to well A1.
4. Transfer 20 µL from row N to row N+1; mix 5–10 times.
5. Discard final 20 µL from the last row after transfer.
6. Use C1V1=C2V2 to verify target concentrations.
